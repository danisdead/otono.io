_ Simplexity


By Rolando Hernández & Enrique Maraver


A collection of short episodes, Simplexity is the merging of a few brief moments lived by two sound artists in their own paths. This is a memory of the lo-fi experiments in field recording done by Enrique Maraver and the in situ live touring of Rolando Hernández: the witnessing not of an especific composition, but rather of the ephemeral fleeting of a few instants in ambient noise and natural aural dins. The result are 10 audio fragments that gather seven recordings of public spaces in Mexico City, and a few that register the live situacionism of Rolando's recent performing with a piece especifically done for an intervened CD Player.


Together, Rolando and Enrique gather these moments of 2014 in a temporary net art experiment for the Otono label, only available as a 24 hour stream. This is the culmination of a year's worth details, previously only experienced by those present, and now released as a digital eclipse that shall only exist in our online memory on tonight's winter solstice.


This is not music, this is the invisible game of sound as it is captured in its magical space.
